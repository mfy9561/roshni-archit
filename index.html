How We Work

01
Discovery & Vision

We begin with deep listening — understanding your lifestyle, aspirations, and the story you want your space to tell.

02
Concept Design

Our team translates your vision into detailed spatial concepts, mood boards, and 3D renderings that bring ideas to life.

03
Design Development

Every material, finish, and fixture is curated with precision — from custom millwork to bespoke furniture sourcing.

Execution & Delivery

Our site team ensures flawless execution, managing every contractor and detail until the keys are in your hands.

The Studio

By Roshani
Ramchandani Sachdev

With over 14 years of experience and more than 400 projects across India, Roshani Architects stands at the intersection of luxury, emotion, and storytelling — shaping environments that transcend their physical form.

Operating from Raipur and Indore, the studio delivers comprehensive end-to-end design solutions rooted in a seamless blend of classical grandeur and modern sophistication.

"Architecture is not just what you see — it's what you feel within a space."
Roshani
